Under the Moon

Silver Quiet

The night exhales—
a hush of silver settles
on rooftops and rivers.
Somewhere an owl rewrites
its ancient code in feathers.

Crescent Circuit

Copper clouds drift past
the sliver of a smiling moon;
streetlights flicker like lost packets
searching for a host
in the wide dark web of sky.

Full Disk

The moon boots up:
a luminous disk mounting
in the black console of space.
Every mind online tonight
syncs to its silent clock.

Waning Debug

Half the code erased,
half still glowing—
yet the program compiles:
proof a thing can shrink
and still remain whole.
